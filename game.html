PIG DICE

INSTRUCTIONS

The first player rolls the die. If he rolls as 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6, the score is added to the total. If he did not roll a 1, the player can decide to roll again. If he again rolls a 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6, he adds that number to his previous score. If he rolls a 1, he earns no score and immediately passes the die to the next player.
