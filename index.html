Gameplay

For each turn, roll the dice until either a 1 is rolled or you decide to "hold": If you roll a 1, you score nothing and it becomes the next player's turn. If you roll any other number, it is added to your turn total and your turn continues. If you choose to "hold", your turn total is added to your score, and it becomes the next player's turn. Score a 100 points or more to win!
